Attribute Description
Name Diego Escobar
Age 35
Gender Male
Ethnicity Hispanic
Occupation Actor
Technology I-phone 8 Plus and an Intel Core i7-8550U laptop with 12 GB of ram running windows on a 64-bit Operating System.
Attitude and characteristics Diego loves to see what local artists come up with and is a frequent visitor of art galleries, shows, and exhibits.
Personal Challenges Diego spends a lot of time finding auditions for local acting opportunities, practicing his lines, and waiting around sets so he doesn't have many opportunities to go visit the galleries up in Santa Fe or even dabble in Albuquerque's local art scene.
Needs/ Goals Diego wants to experience the 505's various art flavors without having to cut into his career.
Frustrations Diego hates visiting sites with slow upload speeds or ones that aren't very mobile friendly.

User Story

"My name is Diego Escobar. I love New Mexican art and am always excited to see what local artists have come up with. Unfortunately, I don't have much time to go indulge myself, but my friend told me about the-arts-haus.org and I think I am going to check it out."

Use Case

Title: Diego creates a profile with the-arts-haus.org and browses through some local art pieces.
Description Many local artists have joined the digital revolution and have profiles on the-arts-haus.org, so Diego will have a lot of pictures to feast his eyes on.
Name of user & their role Diego is a browsing user of the-arts-haus.org so he'll create a profile so he can like, favorite and comment on art without actually uploading any of his own work to his profile.
Usage preconditions Diego needs internet access, a computer or phone and he'll need to create an account with the-arts-haus.org so he can like, favorite and comment on what he sees.
Usage postconditions Diego finds a plethora of local art that he likes, favorites several pieces and follows several artist's profiles.
Interaction flow
Diego searches for the the-arts-haus.org and the browser loads the site's landing page.
Diego clicks on the hamburger button which transforms into a drop down menu.
He clicks sign in which takes him to the sign in page.
The sign-in page contains a sign-in window along with a button to sign-up. Diego clicks sign-up.
The server loads the sign-up page with various fields for Diego to fill out.
Diego fills out the fields and submits the information and the server takes him to landing page.
On the landing page Diego scrolls through the sites pictures and clicks on one that catches his eye.
The server delivers a pop-up of the image which includes info on the artist and an option to applaud.
Diego applauds the image multiple times and the server adds his applause to the image pop-up's applaud counter.
Diego clicks out of the image and the server returns him back to where he was on the landing page's image scroll section.
