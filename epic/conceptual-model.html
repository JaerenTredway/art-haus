imageId primary key

Entity: Gallery
Attribute: notes:
galleryId primary key
galleryProfileId foreign key
galleryDate
galleryName
Entity: Applaud
Attribute: notes:
applaudProfileId foreign key
applaudImageId foreign key
applaudCount
Relations:
1 profile can have many images | 1 : m
1 profile can have many galleries | 1 : m
many profiles can applaud many images | m : 1 : m (applaud is weak entity/ intermediary)
1 gallery can have many images | 1 : m (gallery is weak entity/ intermediary)
