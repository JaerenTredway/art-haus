Persona 3

Attribute Description
Name Gilbert Lowell
Age 26
Gender male
Ethnicity caucasian
Occupation computer programmer
Technology iPhone 6, iOS 12.3; macbook pro, High Sierra, Chrome
Attitude and characteristics Gilbert is a young urban professional who is enjoying life in the Albuquerque tech scene. He's a programmer by trade and is interested in the social life of hip Albuquerqueans.
Personal Challenges Gilbert wants to make more friends and get more 'wired-in' to the Albuquerque art and maker scene.
Needs/ Goals Gilbert uses locallly-connected web resources like MeetUps and Facebook to try to meet more cool people.
Frustrations Gilbert feels like it should be easy to meet people, especially artisans and creative people.

User Story

"My name is Gilbert Lowell and I want to meet cool local artists."

Use Case

Title: Gilbert checks out the-art-haus.org for the first time.
Description Gilbert heard about The Art Haus and is intrigued, he loads the page to see what's up.
Name of user & their role Gilbert, a computer programmer and art/ maker fan who loves to check out interesting stuff that people make.
Usage preconditions All Gilbert needs is internet access and the name The Art Haus.
Usage postconditions Gilbert will either choose to become a member and stay interested in the website, or not.
Interaction flow
Gilbert searches google on his phone for 'The Art Haus' because he doesn't know the web address.
Google provides Albuquerque's the-art-haus.org as it's first search result.
Gilbert taps the link on his phone.
The landing page loads fast, less than 2 seconds.
Gilbert is instantly enchanted by the beautiful and intriguing images of stuff local people have made, and imediately wants an account so he can dive deeper into the galleries.
He effortlessly locates the 'sign-up' page.
He signs up for the-art-haus.org in about 15 seconds.
