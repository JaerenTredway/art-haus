Executive Summary

The Art Haus is a platform for artists and artisans of all persuasions to showcase their creations to their local community. Users are able to view art within 100 miles of their present location, encouraging the support of their local art scene. The Art Haus can be described as Etsy meets Pintrist with a geographic limiter.
