Persona 1

Attribute Description
Name Whitney
Age 19
Gender female
Ethnicity hispanic
Occupation student/artist
Technology 15" HP Laptop (Windows 10 Home, Chrome browser), Android phone
Attitude and characteristics Whitney is a frequent buyer and occasional seller on sites like Etsy and is a frequent user of Facebook and Instagram, but she does not feel like she has a way to showcase her art to her community outside her friend group.
Personal Challenges Whitney is an avid creator, but her confidence is low so she is not ready to show much of her work to a global audience.
Needs/ Goals Whitney needs a way to showcase her art to her local community and access art from other local artists. She wants to be able to support her local art scene and fellow artists.
Frustrations Whitney has difficulty showing her art in a non-commercial way and trouble finding other local artists to collaborate and network with.

User Story

As a user, Whitney needs to have a platform to showcase her art to her local community and receive positive feedback to encourage further creations.

Use Case

Title: Whitney Showcases Her Art To Her Local Art Haus Community
Description Whitney wants to share her new art work with her local community.
Name of user & their role Whitney, a 19-year old artist and student wanting to showcase her artwork.
Usage preconditions Must have profile on The Art Haus, internet access, and images of her art in digital form.
Usage postconditions Image of Whitney's art is uploaded to server and randomized into main gallery.
Interaction flow
Whitney enters the-art-haus.org in her browser.
Server returns Whitney's main Art Haus page.
Whitney selects Profile from hamburger menu at top of page.
Server displays Whitney's profile page.
Whitney selects Upload Art button from profile page.
Server returns image upload page.
Whitney clicks browse and selects desired file from her file explorer.
Whitney enters title and description of art and clicks Submit.
Server uploads file and generates confirmation message.
Whitney clicks Return To Profile button.
Server returns Whitney's profile page.
Uploaded image is visible in Whitney's main album and randomized in main gallery.
